Our Mission :-

The mission of the Computer Science Club at Frisco is to expand our knowledge of the various concepts that fall under the umbrella of Computer Science. We aim to educate and innovate with our collective insight as a student-run organization. With a focus on project-based learning, our hands-on approach will continually evolve to accommodate the changing technological world.
